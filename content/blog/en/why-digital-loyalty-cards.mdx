## The Problem Every Local Business Knows

You've seen it a hundred times. A customer digs through their wallet, past receipts and old cards, looking for that stamp card they swore they had. They can't find it. You hand them a new one. The cycle repeats.

Paper loyalty cards have a fundamental problem: **they rely on customers to keep track of them**. And customers are terrible at it. Studies consistently show that over 50% of paper loyalty cards are lost, forgotten, or thrown away within the first month.

## Why Loyalty Apps Don't Work Either

The tech industry's answer was dedicated loyalty apps. But that created a new problem: **app fatigue**.

Your customers already have dozens of apps on their phone. Asking them to download yet another one — just for stamp cards — is a big ask. The friction kills engagement before it starts. Most loyalty apps see download rates under 10% of eligible customers.

<InfoBox type="tip" title="The real numbers">
  The average smartphone user has 80+ apps installed but only uses about 9 daily. A standalone loyalty app for a single business has almost zero chance of making that daily list.
</InfoBox>

## The Wallet Solution

There's a place on every smartphone that customers already use daily: **their digital wallet**. Apple Wallet and Google Wallet are built into every phone. No download required. No account to create. No password to remember.

When your loyalty card lives in the wallet, it's:

- **Always accessible** — right next to their credit cards and boarding passes
- **Always visible** — wallet passes appear on lock screens near your location
- **Always updated** — push notifications show new stamps in real-time
- **Never lost** — digital passes sync across devices and can be re-downloaded

## How It Works in Practice

The customer experience is dead simple:

1. Customer scans a QR code at your counter
2. One tap adds the loyalty card to their wallet
3. Next visit, they show you the pass — you scan their QR code
4. They get a push notification: "You earned a stamp!"
5. Their pass updates instantly with the new stamp count

No app. No login. No friction. Just scan, tap, done.

## The Business Benefits

For business owners, digital wallet loyalty cards solve problems beyond just "not losing cards":

- **Real data**: Know who visits, how often, and when they're close to a reward
- **Push notifications**: Re-engage customers who haven't visited in a while
- **Zero hardware**: Scan from any phone or browser — no POS integration needed
- **Professional branding**: Your logo, your colors, your business in their wallet
